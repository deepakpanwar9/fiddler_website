FAQ

What is considered a resource?


Cloud resources are entities that are created and managed using the cloud management console, CLI interfaces, SDKs, or configuration management tools.


What is included in the core Bridgecrew platform?


Bridgecrew’s core platform includes our complete policy index, security-as-code remediations, IAM Insights, and more.


Do I need a credit card to try Bridgecrew?


No, you can sign up and start using Bridgecrew without submitting any credit card information.


Is Bridgecrew providing additional support during the COVID-19 pandemic?


We’re providing access to our platform free of charge for six months to organizations experiencing security layoffs and budget cuts, as well as those within the healthcare, hospitality, travel, and entertainment industries. Reach out to support to learn more about this offer.


Are there limits to the number of sources I can connect?


On our Community plan and above, there are no limits to how many infrastructure code repositories you can connect. On our Standard plan and above, there are no limits to how many cloud accounts you can connect.


What are Custom Policies?


In addition to providing out-of-the-box policies and cloud best practices, our paid plans all come with the ability to add custom policies to scan and benchmark against.


How do I upgrade my plan?


If you’re ready to upgrade your plan, contact our team.


Does Bridgecrew sponsor plans for open-source projects?


Yes! In addition to our Community plan which is free for up to 100 resources, Bridgecrew is happy to support projects that are free, open-sourced, publicly accessible to all, and independently operated. To learn more, contact our team.
